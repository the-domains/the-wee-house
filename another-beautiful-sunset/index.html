Wee House

The Wee house will not be conventional in the sense that there will be very little lot clearing, and with the small footprint it will leave the lot as natural as possible. There will not be driving on the lot, the Wee house will have a walkway that will wind up the hill, and since we aren’t leveling the lot, we wont need flood insurance. This keeps our insurance cost to a minimum. The total amount needed to fund this project is $ 100,000

Interest = $ 100,000 @ 8% =$ 670 per month or $ 8000 per year
